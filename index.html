About Me

I'm currently a Research Fellow at WING @ NUS, supervised by Prof. Min-Yen Kan. I'm also affiliated with NUSRI-CQ, supervised by Prof. Zhiyong Huang. My research field includes multilingual NLP and ML for NLP. I obtained my PhD at Chongqing University, my MSc at the University of Edinburgh, and my BSc at Northumbria University. Before pursuing my PhD, I worked for 3 years at a start-up company. My lab encountered challenges with a chatbot project (before the advent of GPT) in the quarantine time, which prompted me to return to university for further training. I found a PhD program in my hometown. When I'm not working, I'm often palying basketball.
